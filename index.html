Welcome to our Institute

Srijan Private Industrial Training Institute has been established in the year 20-January-2015. Srijan ITI has been in the forefront imparting high-quality technical education and is one among the top ten ITI colleges in Muzaffarpur, Bihar. State of the art infrastructure in all trades (Electrician and Fitter), dedicated and qualified staff, highly conducive environment for teaching-learning process and a lush green campus are the hallmarks of this professionally managed institution.
